# Project Brief

**GMaker** is a specialized "Growth Partner Automatizado" SaaS designed for US Immigration Lawyers targeting the Latino market. 

**Core Value Proposition**: The "Autopilot" engine. GMaker connects to the client's Google Ads account and acts as a set-and-forget orchestrator that automatically pauses wasteful keywords (Loss Prevention) and recommends scaling opportunities. 

**Business Model**: Pricing is tiered based strictly on the monthly volume of Ad Spend managed by the platform ($49 to $1000+/mo), aligning the SaaS's success directly with the client's marketing budget. Features are not gated; all clients receive the full Autopilot capability.
